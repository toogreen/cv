Airline Inventory Management System

SOFTAIR™ is a complete inventory management system for small to medium air carriers.

The development of SOFTAIR started in 1995 at Royal Airlines, at that time a major charter carrier in Canada. SOFTAIR became the exclusive system at Royal to manage all of their contracts with over 45 tour-operators around the world. SOFTAIR managed electronically, through a very sophisticated network, all the booking and contract related transactions.

In 1998, Royal became, with an aggressive Canadian domestic program, a schedule airline. SOFTAIR’s interface with the CRS allowed Royal to became a true player in the schedule Airline world without loosing their “charter” efficiency.

In 1999, the SOFTAIR Web was introduced to the public (B2C) and to Travel agencies (B2B). After few months, 35% of Royal Domestic sales were generated through this distribution method.

SOFTAIR was implemented successfully at Air Transat in 1998, and certain modules of the system were installed at Canada 3000 during the same year.

The following is a list of SOFTAIR™ main features:

  • Reservations Management
  • Seat Selection
  • Yield Management
  • CRS Interface
  • Web Integrated Distribution
  • Accounting / Invoicing Functions
  • Sales Reporting

Features and Benefits

The following provides a list and description of the features and benefits of SMRS™.

Reservations:
SOFTAIR™ host server supports connectivity with all Global Distribution Systems (GDS) and the Web. Softvoyage’s technological advancements transmit seat allocations directly to Tour Operators and GDS. Reservation details are electronically returned to the airline company by the same method of communication. SOFTAIR™ reservation module allows the capture of clients’ seat selections and other special requests.

Parameters Customization:
SOFTAIR™ allows to maintain parameter tables used throughout the system, create user codes and define dynamic security.
Yield Management:
Softvoyage provides an innovative method to manage airline prices. Our “Yield Management” utility automatically assesses sales and revenues, calculating and readjusting loads to maximize revenue.
CRS Interface:Our proven interface allows automatic distribution of seat allotments to GDS and uploading of passenger name records (PNR) directly from the GDS to SOFTAIR™.
Web Integrated Distribution:Our unparalleled Internet flexibility allows both Travel Agents and the public to make reservations and select seats directly with the airline.
Accounting / invoicing functions:Maintain accounting information (Rate Tables, Supplement & Reductions, Client’s commission, Taxes…) required to invoice clients according to their sales and conditions. Automatically fax ‘Price Grids’ and, eventually, update price tables in the client system.
Reporting:SOFTAIR™ enables the creation of over 40 reports, from seats load to complete revenue/cost analysis by city pair, departure, season can be produce:

  • Product Revenue reports based on the costs (by flight and itinerary) and the actual sales
  • Produce miscellaneous reports for analysis and inventory control
  • Generate seating charts
  • Produce APNL reports
Technology & Hosting:Our solutions can be installed locally at your premises or hosted and delivered remotely to maximize speed of execution, performance, and costs sharing opportunities.

For ASP clients, our server room is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, connectivity and monitoring devices so your customers enjoy a fast, stable, and secure experience. Softvoyage uses 128-bit SSL encryption, the industry’s strongest security standard.

SOFTAIR™ is built on the SAX™ platform using the XML standard language which provides the most flexibility and robustness in today’s interconnected world.

Customer Support:SOFTAIR™ is supported by Softvoyage first-class customer support programs which is available during normal business hours.

Hotel Management & Distribution System

QUARTO™ is a highly customizable hotel management system allowing suppliers complete control over hotel accommodation distribution.

Our comprehensive system enables suppliers to allocate accommodation among selected wholesale Tour Operators and effectively manage booking and payment activity. Complete system tasks room pricing and payment transactions are monitored while adjusting inventories automatically. Our Internet connectivity capability enables suppliers with an effective communication media with which to manage a multitude of hotels with various accommodations.

QUARTO™ modular architecture provides our customers flexibility. You only choose the components you need and we customize the parameters so they best address the very specifications of your operations.

QUARTO™ is a comprehensive hotel system that manages every facet of the accommodation process. The system performs a wide range of tasks covering the whole spectrum of the booking and room allotment process, from the initial contract and inventory creation to final customer check-in.

The following is a list of QUARTO™ main features:

  • Complete hotel management;
  • Infinite accommodation scenarios;
  • Client profile maintenance;
  • Contract and inventory management;
  • Partial and full payments capture and process;
  • System Parameters;
  • Price management;
  • Reservation control;
  • Regulation for exceptional situations (i.e. stop sale, cancellation);
  • Comprehensive module defining supplements and bonuses;
  • User access management/security settings;
  • Customer check in module;
  • Inventory verification;
  • Integrated accounting;
  • Management reporting.

Features & Benefits

The following provides a list and description of the features and benefits of QUARTO™.

Manage All Types of Accommodations:QUARTO™ allows you to manage all types of hotel product and distribute them seamlessly under one environment. Under the same platform various types of accommodation may be created allowing for room requirements, child age specifications and price details.
Complete Client Profile:Complete client profiles are managed by QUARTOTM This informational database will allow you to manage client contact details, currency and language requirements.
Contract Management & Inventory Tracking:QUARTO™ provides you with great features:

  • The inventory is generated automatically from the contract details and is verified in real-time.
  • The system can handle unique situations like stop sale, cut offs, cancellations, supplemental fees and bonus programs can also be managed effectively.
  • Rooms are priced for client distribution in an efficient and concise method using easily accessed price panels. QUARTO™ also provides the ability to manage special pricing situations including child age requirements, extra occupancy and gateway details.
  • Reservation details from wholesale Tour Operators are controlled by booking batch entries detailing passenger booking information.
  • Inventories are automatically adjusted and controlled with a robust inventory verification module. Pricing and client payments are automatically regulated including fee and bonus stipulations.
Friendly Reservation / Check-In Module:This module manages client reservations and check-in in a user-friendly environment. Passenger information that is stored in the hotel booking database is automatically accessed to confirm check-in status.
Complete Accounting Module:This module effectively handles all of your accounting needs:

  • Client invoicing and payments
  • Payment inquiry
  • Extensive reports and financial summaries, including client invoicing and account statement details
  • Accommodation control, including cut off and room status reports
Web Integrated Distribution:QUARTO™ supports secure Internet distribution which is an essential distribution channel for travel products. Because it is fully integrated to the application, our customers can maintain on single data source and avoid costly and problematic duplication. Softvoyage XML application program interfaces (API) can also enable the distribution of your products to various travel stakeholders, including the allotment to wholesale Tour Operators.

For ASP clients, our server room is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, connectivity and monitoring devices so your customers enjoy a fast, stable, and secure experience. Softvoyage uses 128-bit SSL encryption, the industry’s strongest security standard.

QUARTO™ is built on the SAX™ platform using the XML standard language which provides the most flexibility and robustness in today’s interconnected world.

Technology & Hosting:Our solutions can be installed locally at your premises or hosted and delivered remotely to maximize speed of execution, performance, and costs sharing opportunities.
Customer Support:QUARTO™ is supported by Softvoyage first-class customer support programs which is available during normal business hours.
