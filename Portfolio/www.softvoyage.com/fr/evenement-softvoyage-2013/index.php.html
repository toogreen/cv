Updates from Softvoyage

Softvoyage Event in Montréal

Softvoyage is proud to be returning to the Biosphère to offer specialized workshops to the people of the travel industry.

On May 6, Softvoyage is proposing 4 workshops that will be presented in the afternoon:

Workshop 1:
The Travel Industry
Overview of the market and tendencies.
Target audience: Everyone

Workshop 2:
SIREV
Discover all the new features, the possibilities and advantages of the various search and booking platforms.
Target audience: Travel agents, owners and managers of agencies

Workshop 3:
Web Modules
Make sure that you have a complete transactional site that includes Packages, Flights, Hotels and Tours.
Target audience: Owners and managers of agencies, tour operators, tourism offices, travel agents

Workshop 4:
Monarc and Advertising
Discover the advantages of Monarc as well as all the advertising tools that are available to reach travel agents.
Target audience: Owners and managers of agencies, tour operators, tourism offices, hotel managers, suppliers of tourism products

The Biosphere site is ideal for this type of event. We hope to have a great turn-out!

All details and the registration form are on this website : http://event.softvoyage.com/

Hope to see you there!

The Softvoyage Team
