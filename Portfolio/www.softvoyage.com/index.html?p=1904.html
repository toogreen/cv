Monarc Top Ranking 2012



Dan Langevin, Vice President of Sales and Marketing at Softvoyage travelled to Jamaica and presented the Iberostar Rose Hall Suites with their multiple awards for the Jamaica region. Pictured here are: Ms. Celia Ferran – Resident Manager, Mr. Klaus Frauenschlager – Executive Chef, Mrs. Sonja Nalkiran – General Manager, Dan Langevin – Softvoyage, Ms. Toffel Fletcher – Guest Service Manager and Mr. Desmond Douglas – Maitre d’

Monarc.ca is pleased to announce its ranking of the most popular hotels with Canadian travelers, as well as the name of winners by category and specific criteria.

Montreal – March 21st, 2013… Monarc.ca, the only Canadian Hotel review site for Canadian travelers, is pleased to publish its ranking of the most popular hotels in sun destinations for Canadian travelers for the year 2012.

Now in its fourth year, the site www.monarc.ca contains more than 75 000 travelers’ reviews and comments coming from all Canadian provinces. It is the third consecutive year that a classification based on Canadian traveler’s comments and reviews is available to the general public.

In this ranking, we find the most popular hotels based on specific criteria, such as overall impression of the establishment, the restaurants, the beaches, value for your money, as a family vacation or a couples destination. Monarc.ca also released a classification of the best hotels by destination.

These rankings take into account the following destinations:
Antigua and Barbuda, Aruba, Bahamas, Barbados, Bermuda, Colombia, Costa Rica, Cuba, Curacao, Guadeloupe, Grenada, Honduras, Cayman Islands, Jamaica, Martinique, Mexico, Nicaragua, Panama, Dominican Republic, Saint Lucia, St Maarten, Turks and Caicos, and Venezuela.

The www.monarc.ca site offers travelers a high quality site that guarantees the integrity of its content. Only travelers who have purchased a trip from a participating Canadian travel agency can post a review on the hotel they stayed at. Monarc.ca is a property of Softvoyage Inc.

About Softvoyage
Since 1987, Softvoyage has been powering the leisure travel industry by delivering innovative and robust IT and distribution solutions. Softvoyage solutions enable the management, distribution and procurement of travel services for hundreds of North-American Travel Agencies, Tour Operators & Consolidators, and Travel Suppliers. Some of the major systems developed by Softvoyage are SIREV (shopping system for travel agents), SIRRVA & MRUN (web booking engine for travelers), TTS (inventory management, reservations and distribution for tour operators), Gulliver (tool for managing negotiated fares for consolidators) and Softair (reservation system for airlines). Softvoyage has offices in Montreal, Laval and Toronto, and sales representatives in Europe and the United States.
